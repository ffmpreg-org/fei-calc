Calculate the voltage across resistor R4 based on the series-parallel connection of resistors R1, R2, R3, R4 and voltage Ui.

$$
R_{12} = R_1 + R_2, \quad R_{123} = \frac{R_{12} \times R_3}{R_{12} + R_3}, \quad R_{1234} = R_{123} + R_4
$$

$$
\quad U_4 = U_i \times \frac{R_4}{R_{1234}}
$$
