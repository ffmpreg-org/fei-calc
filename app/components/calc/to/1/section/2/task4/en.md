Calculate the short-circuit current on terminals A and B based on the series-parallel connection of resistors Ri, R1, R2 and current Ii. Resistor R3 is disconnected from the circuit, it is not included in the calculation.

$$
R_{i1} = \frac{R_i \times R_1}{R_i + R_1}
$$

$$
I_2 = I_i \times \frac{R_{i1}}{R_{i1} + R_2}
$$
